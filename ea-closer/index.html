THE ROLE

What this is about

We have helped over 4,500 people build or scale their marketing agencies. People come to us because they are ready to grow further and they want the right support to do it. We believe every aspiring entrepreneur deserves the chance to succeed, and that belief drives everything we do.

Your role is simple. Qualified prospects book strategy calls. You get on those calls, understand where they are, and close the right people into the programme. You are not selling something people don't need. You are helping them make a decision that could change their trajectory.

You will be part of a close-knit team and coaching is built into the role. We invest in making you better. If you show up and do the work, you will grow fast here.

RESPONSIBILITIES

What you will do

  • Take qualified strategy calls with interested prospects
  • Run the full sales process: discovery, presenting, objection handling, closing
  • Hit monthly revenue targets consistently
  • Keep accurate deal notes and pipeline data in the CRM
  • Attend coaching sessions, review your calls, and implement feedback
  • Share learnings with the team to raise the bar for everyone

REQUIREMENTS
