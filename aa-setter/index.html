The role

What this is about

We have helped 4,500 people build or scale their agencies. Every month, hundreds more apply to join Affluent Academy. We need someone to talk to them first.

Your job is simple. People apply for the programme. You call them, find out where they are, and decide whether they are a fit. The right ones get booked in with a closer. The wrong ones get told why now is not the time.

You are the first voice every applicant hears. That matters. Get this right and the closer's job is half done before they pick up the phone.

Responsibilities

What you will do

  • Call inbound applicants the moment their application lands. Every one is a warm lead, and speed wins. A lead called in five minutes closes far more often than one called an hour later.
  • Run qualification calls: where they are, what they want, what they can afford, whether now is the right time.
  • Decide which tier of Affluent Academy fits each applicant and route them accordingly. You own the tier call.
  • Book qualified applicants onto a closer's calendar with full context handed over.
  • Reschedule no-shows and recover anyone who drops out before the close call. Nobody falls through the cracks on your watch.
  • Keep accurate notes and pipeline data in the CRM after every conversation.
  • Attend coaching sessions, review your own calls, and implement feedback.
The right person

Who we are looking for

You read people fast

You can spend ten minutes on the phone with someone and know if they are serious. You ask the right questions and you actually listen to the answers.

You move quickly

When an application lands, you call within minutes. You treat every warm lead like it is the only one. Speed and follow-up are how you win.

You take coaching

You review your own calls. You take feedback seriously. You want to be better this month than you were last month and you do something about it.

Requirements

What you need

  • Phone-based sales or qualification experience: setting, screening, inbound sales, recruiting, or similar
  • Comfortable on the phone for four or more hours a day
  • You can run a qualification conversation: ask, listen, decide, book or close out
  • Good judgement on price and fit. You can tell a serious buyer from a tyre-kicker quickly
  • You are based in the UK timezone or are available to work those hours (preferred)
  • You manage yourself and hit your booking targets without needing to be chased
Be honest with yourself

Do not apply if...

This role is not for everyone. If any of the below sounds like you, save us both the time.

✕

You hate the phone. This role is the phone. If picking up makes you nervous, you will not enjoy a single day of it.

✕

You think setting is reading a script. Every applicant is different. You have to think on the call, not dial through a list on autopilot.

✕

You want a guaranteed salary with no accountability. This is commission only. You earn what you book and what closes. The ceiling is high if you are good.
