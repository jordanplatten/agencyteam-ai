THE ROLE

What this is about

Appointwise is an AI-powered appointment setting platform used by businesses to automate and scale their outbound sales. We are growing fast and we need someone who can accelerate that growth.

This is not a typical SDR role. Your day-to-day is roughly 80% cold and warm outreach and 20% nurturing existing strategic partnerships. You will be using a multitude of strategies, including maintaining a multi-channel AI system that uses email, social media DMs, and other channels to generate conversations at scale. Hundreds of outreaches per day, the kind of volume a human alone cannot sustain.

You must have experience using AI to build outreach systems. The role is benchmarked on new revenue, which is also how your commission is calculated. There are real progression opportunities here for the right person.

RESPONSIBILITIES

What you will do

  • Build, manage, and optimise multi-channel AI outreach systems across email, social media DMs, and other channels
  • Execute high-volume cold and warm outbound prospecting daily, hundreds of touches per day
  • Identify, reach out to, and nurture strategic partnership opportunities
  • Book qualified demos and meetings that convert into new revenue
  • Track pipeline, attribution, and conversion data to optimise your approach
  • Work directly with the co-founders to refine outreach strategy and messaging

  • Available for US or UK business hours
  • Self-starter who takes full ownership of their pipeline and results
